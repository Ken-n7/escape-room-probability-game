ESCAPE ROOM: PROBABILITY CHALLENGE

PURPOSE

An interactive 3D escape room game designed as an educational intervention for Grade 8 students studying Simple Probability. Learners solve probability problems to escape from haunted classrooms.

DIFFICULTY LEVELS

Room 1 — Easy: Introduces basic probability concepts, definitions, and key terms.

Room 2 — Moderate: Applies the probability formula with given favorable and total outcomes.

Room 3 — Hard: Solves real-life word problems by independently identifying data and computing probability.

FEATURES

▶ Play — Full escape room challenge across 3 difficulty levels.

🎲 P-Learn — Reviews discussions, examples, and the probability formula before gameplay.

⚙ Settings — Edit your profile name and track your room scores.

BENEFITS FOR STUDENTS

Builds conceptual understanding through game-based learning, promotes self-paced review, and develops problem-solving skills in a motivating, low-anxiety environment.
